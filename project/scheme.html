Gold Savings Schemes

Purchase the wedding and investment jewellery of your dreams systematically with zero making/wastage charges and Rate Protection benefits.

Daily Gold Scheme

330 Days

Save small amounts daily. Every deposit is instantly booked and converted into physical gold weight based on that day's rate, shielding you from inflation.

Weekly Gold Scheme

52 Weeks

Systematic weekly gold booking. Lock in fixed amounts weekly and purchase ornaments upon maturity with exciting zero-wastage perks.

Monthly Gold Scheme

11 Months

Our classic flagship savings scheme. Save fixed sums monthly, book gold systematically on payment dates, and enjoy full wastage waiver benefits.

Daily Gold Scheme Rules

  • Minimum Savings: The minimum daily saving amount is ₹100.
  • Gold Conversion: Cash payments are immediately converted into gold weight based on current market rates.
  • Duration: The duration of the scheme is exactly 330 days.
  • Redemption: Customers can redeem accumulated gold weight only as gold ornaments.
  • Applicable Billing: GST, making, wastage, and stone charges apply on redemption.
  • No Refund: Cash refund is strictly not allowed.
  • Jurisdiction: All transactions are subject to Karaikudi jurisdiction.

Weekly & Monthly Scheme Rules

  • Fixed Installments: Choose from ₹500, ₹1,000, ₹1,500, ₹2,000, ₹2,500, ₹3,000, ₹5,000, ₹7,000, or ₹10,000.
  • Duration: Weekly scheme runs for 52 weeks. Monthly scheme runs for 11 months.
  • Gold Rate Booking: Gold weight is calculated based on the market rate on the date of each payment.
  • Scheme Benefit: No making charges or wastage charges (Vazhipadu) apply on accumulated gold weight upon maturity.
  • GST: 3% GST is payable on billing.
  • Early Discontinuation: No cash refund. In case of early closure, customer can purchase ornaments only for the amount paid, and scheme benefits (wastage waiver) are not applicable.
  • Early Closure Penalty: Early closure attracts normal making and wastage charges.
  • Extra Purchase: Purchasing jewellery beyond accumulated gold attracts normal making and wastage charges.
  • Payment Limit: Only one payment is allowed per payment period (week or month). Missed payments cannot be combined.
  • Jurisdiction: Subject to Karaikudi jurisdiction.
